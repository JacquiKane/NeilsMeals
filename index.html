Chicken & Mashed Potatoes
Mashed Potatoes & Chicken
stars3 large potatoes?
stars Chicken breast
stars Chicken breast
stars Butter
stars Cream, half & half or milk
stars Mixed veggies or salad
 question_answer

Add oil and seasoning (your choice) to a pan. Warm, add the chicken breast and salt and pepper. Fry until done, turning regularly.

Cover the potatoes with water, about 1/2 inch above the potatoes. Bring the potatoes to a boil and add 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Boil the potatoes for 20 to 30 minutes or until they are very easily pierced with a fork and the skin pulls away from the potato where cut.

Drain the potatoes, and while still hot, use a paper towel to wipe the skin off the potatoes, then add back to the warm pot.

Mash potatoes and add the butter. Cover with a lid for the butter to melt (about 3-4 minutes).

Add the half and half (or cream or milk) to the pot and cream the potatoes until smooth. Add salt and black pepper to taste.

Cook the veggies or make the salad.
Quick Chicken Fettucine Alfredo
Chicken Fettucine
starsChicken breasts
starsJar Alfredo sauce

 question_answer

Add oil and seasoning (Italian, or just salt and pepper) to pan and warm. Cut the chicken into strips and add to pan. Fry until the chicken is cooked.

Boil Fettucine (or any other pasta) in salty water.

Drain pasta and add jar of Alfredo sauce. Stir in the chicken.

Instead of the pasta you can use cubed potatoes, or instant rice. You can save any leftover chicken and use the next day with a different base, like rice or quinoa.
Yats Drunken Chicken
Replica Recipe, Drunken CHicken
stars 1 bottle Pale Ale
stars 1/4 cup coconut or olive oil
stars 1 can (12 oz) stewed tomatoes
stars 2 teaspoons Cayenne Pepper
stars 1 teaspoon black pepper
stars 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
stars 1 teaspoon Chili Powder
stars 1 teaspoon Paprika
stars 1 teaspoon onion powder
stars 1 teaspoon garlic powder
stars 1 teaspoon thyme
stars 1 teaspoon basil
stars 1 teaspoon sage
stars 1/2 teaspoon gumbo file
stars 3/4 cups breadcrumbs
stars 2 bay Leaves
stars 1 pound diced chicken breast
stars White or brown rice, or pasta or bread
 question_answer

This is easy to prepare, but requires specific ingredients - you can get these once and they last a long time.

You can freeze it in portions and have it ready to eat. Keep Yats style garlic bread in the freezer too, or make it with a baguette and some garlic butter.

If this turns out too spicy, you can add cream or half and half, to taste. If it's not spicy enough, you can add Louisiana Hot sauce over individual servings.

The original receipe has 1 teaspoon of molasses, which can be added if needed. We didn't appreciate teh taste so we usually leave it out.

Turn slow cooker, pressure cooker, instant pot or dutch oven, to low settings (like 4 to 8 hours).

Pour in beer, coconut oil or olive oil and stewed tomatoes.

Add all of the spices and stir.

Add the cubed chicken and cook on low for 3 to 6 hours. When done, remove the Bay leaves.

Now add the bread crumbs. Note, you can make your own bread crumbs by putting slices of bread or bagels in the liquidizer.

Cook either white or brown long-grain rice, or fried or baked cubed potatoes to serve with the drunken chicken.
